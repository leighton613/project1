Motivation

Due to moving or graduation, many Columbia students tend to sell their second-hand stuff but hardly find a proper platform or find it difficult to reach to someone interested.

Inspired by that, we propose to develop a website which provides a platform to sell and purchase pre-owned products. The basic features for registered users are operations "seller posts a product like textbook" and "buyer orders a posted product like lamp". To improve user experience, users are able to "apply coupon in the order", and "buyers write feedback like 5 starts on seller through order" on this purchasing experience. To standardize product description and eliminate ambiguity, database stores standard information including original price, size, brand, weight and links from Amazon.
